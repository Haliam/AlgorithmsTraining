Summer Sales challenge from CodinGame
https://www.codingame.com/home

It's almost the summer Sales!
You work for a shop that wishes to give a discount of discount% to the most expensive item 
purchased by a gyven customer during the sales period. 
Only one product can benefit from discount.

You are tasked by the shop owner to implement the method CalculateTotalPrice(prices, discount) 
which takes the list of prices of products purchased by a customer and the percentage discount as a parameters 
and return the total purchase price as integer (rounded down if the total is a float number). 

Constraints: 
0 <= discount <= 100
0 < price of a product < 100000
0 < number of products < 100
